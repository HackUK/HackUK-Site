Our Mission

Empowering students across the United Kingdom through free, inclusive, and impactful hackathons.

At HackUK, we believe in fostering creativity, collaboration, and innovation. Our mission is to provide students with the resources and opportunities to build meaningful projects, develop teamwork skills, and create lasting memories. By hosting free hackathons, we aim to break barriers and make technology accessible to everyone.

Join us in our journey to inspire the next generation of problem solvers and innovators.
